VALORANT

An Overview Of: The Sentinal

by NA Libo-on

Sentinels are one of the four roles that agents are divided into in VALORANT. The abilities of agents under this role focus on defensive play,
with emphasis on supporting the rest of the team through providing intel, anchoring down sites, and preventing the enemy from flanking.

As of October 2025, VALORANT currently has 7 Sentinals
