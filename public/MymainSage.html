My Main Sentinal: Sage

Sage is my favorite agent in valorant and my most played agent. She is one of the first sentinals added to valorant. Coming from china, her abilities are more suited towards a defensive playstyle. These include a healing orb that can heal teammates, a slowing orb that can slow enemies, and a barrier orb that can create a wall to block enemy movement and line of sight. Her ultimate ability allows her revive one dead teammate. I love playing as sage because of her abilities are suited to my playstyle. Having the ability to heal and revive teammates makes me very valuable in team fights while not being in the frontlines. Her barriers can also block enemy movement and line of sight, providing strategic advantages for my team. Additionally, her slowing abilities can control enemy movement and create opportunities for my team to capitalize on.


Tips on playing Sage

  1. Always keep an eye on your teammates' health and be ready to heal or revive them when needed.
  2. Use your barrier orb strategically to block enemy movement and line of sight, especially when defending a site.
  3. Use your slowing orb to control enemy movement and create opportunities for your team to capitalize on.
  4. Communicate with your team and coordinate your abilities with theirs to maximize their effectiveness.
  5. Be aware of your positioning and avoid being caught out of position, as Sage is vulnerable to flanking and aggressive pushes.
